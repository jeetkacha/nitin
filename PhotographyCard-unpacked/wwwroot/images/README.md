# Images Folder

## Required Images:

1. **logo.png** - Your business logo (recommended: 300x300px, circular)
2. **qr-code.png** - QR code linking to your website (recommended: 500x500px)

## How to Generate QR Code:

Visit any of these free QR code generators:
- https://www.qr-code-generator.com/
- https://www.qrcode-monkey.com/
- https://qr.io/

Enter your website URL: `https://hinaphotography.com`
Download the PNG image and save it here as `qr-code.png`

## Placeholder Instructions:

Until you add your real images, the app will show broken image icons.
Replace these files with your actual logo and QR code before deploying.
